Hi. I am Akshata.


Engineer and hobbyist thinker, based in Singapore. Orginally from India, I graduated from IIT Kharagpur with a degree in Architecture. In my second year, I coded my way out of a boring an automatable internship and never looked back.

In 2015, I moved to Singapore to join the Design Automation Lab, at the National University of Singapore. I led development for Mobius and Studionet - open source web tools, to make code intuitive and fun!

After three years of research papers, web graphics and parametric design, I continued my love for technology at Viki to bring fullstack joy to over a million K-drama fans, followed by crunching numbers (I led Data Engineering!) for the world's creators at NasAcademy.

Currently, I lead tech and product for Metaphi and Awen Finance. At Awen, we are building to empower SMEs and solve for the $1.7T trade finance gap in the world - a comprehensive suite of Trade Finance payment products, risk analytics and tokenization of trade assets on-chain.

I also organise Practical Blockchain - an online group of Blockchain enthusiasts, to meet on monthly basis for smart contract workshops.

We are hiring engineers at Awen! Reach out at akshata@metaphi.xyz.
